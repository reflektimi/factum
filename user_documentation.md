# User Guide - Finances SaaS

Welcome to **Finances SaaS**, your complete solution for business financial management. This guide will help you navigate the platform and make the most of its features.

---

## 1. Getting Started

### Logging In
Access the platform via your provided URL. Enter your email and password. If you are a new user, an administrator must create an account for you.

### Dashboard
The **Dashboard** is your command center.
- **Metrics**: View total revenue, outstanding invoices, and expense totals at a glance.
- **Charts**: Visualise your income vs. expenses over time.
- **Activity Feed**: See the latest actions taken by your team.

---

## 2. Managing Contacts (Accounts)

The **Accounts** module serves as your address book for Customers and Suppliers.
- **Create Account**: Click "Create Account", enter the name, email, and type (Customer/Supplier).
- **View Details**: Click on a name to see their contact info, current balance, and transaction history.
- **Archive**: Remove an account from active lists without deleting their historical data.

---

## 3. Invoicing

### Creating an Invoice
1. Go to **Invoices** > **Issue Invoice**.
2. **Select Customer**: Choose from your existing accounts.
3. **Set Dates**: Issue date and Due date.
4. **Add Items**: List services or products with quantity and price.
5. **Save**:
    - **Draft**: Save to work on later.
    - **Sent**: Mark as finalized and sent to the client.

### Managing Invoices
- **Status Filtering**: Use the filter dropdown to see only `Unpaid`, `Overdue`, or `Draft` invoices.
- **Record Payment**: Open an invoice and click "Add Payment" to log a transaction.
- **Export**: enhanced specific invoices as PDF for printing or emailing.

---

## 4. Recurring Billing
Automate your regular subscriptions or retainers.
1. Go to **Recurring Invoices**.
2. Create a profile (e.g., "Monthly SEO Retainer").
3. Set the **Interval** (Weekly, Monthly, Yearly).
4. The system will automatically generate standard invoices based on this schedule.

---

## 5. Expenses
Track where your money is going.
- **Log Expense**: Enter the amount, date, description, and merchant.
- **Categorize**: Tags expenses as `Office`, `Travel`, `Software`, etc., for better reporting.
- **Receipts**: (Optional) Upload a digital copy of the receipt for tax compliance.

---

## 6. Payments & Credit Notes

### Payments
- View a master list of all incoming money.
- Filter by method (`Credit Card`, `Bank Transfer`, `Cash`).

### Credit Notes
- Issue refunds or store credit.
- Apply these against future invoices to reduce the balance due.

---

## 7. Team Management (Users)
*Admin only*
- **Add Member**: Invite new staff.
- **Roles**:
    - **Admin**: Full access.
    - **Manager**: Can manage finances but not system settings.
    - **Accountant**: Read-only access for reporting.

---

## Need Help?
If you encounter technical issues, please contact your system administrator or support team.
